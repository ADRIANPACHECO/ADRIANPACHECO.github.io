Hello!

I'm Adrian Pacheco, a freelance designer currently based in Seattle, Washington. I design simple user-centered experiences for screens of all sizes. Much of my experience as a designer has been influenced by my early life in hardware and construction. That was a time where I found a great appreciation for magazines that were filled with hundreds of house plans. Each house plan showed floor dimensions and mockup photos of what the exterior and interior may look like when built. That type of planning and vision blew my mind!

Around the same time, I saw Apple products as the best computers anyone can own. But it was more than just the computers, it was actually the way that they presented their products to us that inspired me to work with computers in the first place. I went into college thinking that a computer science degree would instantly guide me towards my goal of working with computers for a living. It turned out not being that simple at all! Writing code in Java pointed me away and design took its place, along with coffee, art, and music.
