HELLO,

My name is Adrian Pacheco and I'm a Freelance Designer based in Seattle. I design web and mobile products for growing brands and businesses. I recently graduated from the University of Washinton and earned a Bachelor of Science in Informatics (Human-Computer Interaction).

My passion for computers began as early as Dial-up Internet and manifested during my years of drawing on Microsoft Paint, listening to music on an MP3 player and making my Myspace page look good with CSS. Getting my hands on any product with a digital screen led me into a community of like-minded people, which then led me into the beautiful world of Design.

Since then I have designed for non-profit organizations, startups, and local businesses. I strive for my work to be timeless by making sure every detail exists for a reason. Simple design is what I care about. I also care about writing, reading, and discovering new music over a cup of coffee. : )
