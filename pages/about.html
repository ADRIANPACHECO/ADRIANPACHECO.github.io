ADRIAN PACHECO

I am designer based in Seattle. I strive to design simplistic experiences that challenge the way we intersect technology and human values. I am passionate about solving problems creatively to enlighten perspectives.

Who I am.


I am currently a Senior at the University of Washington studying Informatics, with a focus in Human-Computer Interaction. I come from a family of four who are all creatives, makers, and artists. All of my accomplishments throughout my lifetime have been achieved by having a positive perspective, a focus on quality performance, and a desire for precision. I consistently apply the essence of minimalism into each and any project I work on. I often find myself making decisions based on well-designed elements wherever I am.

What I do.


I was an early User Experience / User Interface Designer for FitTraction, before my current position as a Web Designer for the University District Street Medicine in Seattle. When I am not designing, I enjoy spending my time reading non-fiction books, taking photos, and locating the best happy hours. I love to carry conversations about the stars and our universe. I document much of my experiences in multiple journals that are written in first, second, and third person points of views.
