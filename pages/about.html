Adrian Pacheco: User Experience and User Interface Designer based in Seattle.

Hello! I am a designer focused on user experience design and user interface design. I strive to create timeless digital solutions and simplistic experiences by adopting minimalist design principles into my work.


I have interned at a Seattle startup called FitTraction, where I helped shape the mobile experience design so users can get connected with fitness advisors that motivate and monitor workout progress. After FitTraction, I have worked on various design projects for small organizations as a Freelance Designer. I have recently earned a Bachelors of Science degree in Informatics (HCI) from the University of Washington and am currently looking for a full-time position as UX/UI Designer in the Seattle area.

When I'm not designing, you can catch me reading, golfing, and at happy hours or concerts. You can follow me on LinkedIn, Twitter, Medium, or reach out by email at adrianpachecodesign@gmail.com.
