Miguel Designs brand identities, print, interaction, and signage for creative artists, businesses, and family. I designed and developed a responsive website to highlight the designer's client work while using the Miguel Designs brand identity.

The website uses a grid system to separate and group project assets simultaneously. Image assets that reach the end of its grid-box bleed over to others next to it, giving the work page a creative look and feel. Hovering over a project creates a small moment of enlightenment that motivates users to explore the rest of the page.
